What is Scilab Toolbox Hackathon?

Scilab Toolbox Hackathon is a non-stop “Scilab toolbox development” competition initiated by the FOSSEE, IIT Bombay. Scilab Toolbox Hackathon aims at inspiring young minds to build unique problem-specific toolboxes for the proposed ideas or even open-ended real-life problem(s). Please find the brochure for the hackathon here. You can share it with people interested in the event.

Who can participate?

Anyone who is well acquainted with the functional aspects of Scilab and Xcos or similar numerical computation software can participate in this hackathon. In this challenge, we invite the participation of students, faculty, research scholars, Scilab experts, professionals, etc, across the globe. The participation can be through teams. The team size can range from 1 to 4 members in the team. Ideally, a team can comprise of two domain experts and two coding experts. Apart from the team members, the teams can optionally have a mentor also.

Rules and Reagulations

  • There will be only one point of contact (the team leader) with the organizers.
